Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our mental health assessment services.

How confidential is my assessment?

Your privacy is our top priority. All assessments are completely anonymous and we do not store any personal identifying information. Your results are for your personal use only.

Is this assessment a substitute for professional help?

No, our assessment is designed to be a screening tool and should not replace professional medical advice. If you're experiencing severe symptoms, please consult with a mental health professional.

How accurate are the results?

Our assessment uses the PHQ-9, a scientifically validated screening tool used by healthcare professionals worldwide. However, it's important to discuss any concerns with a qualified mental health professional.

Can I retake the assessment?

Yes, you can retake the assessment as often as you'd like. Many people find it helpful to track their mental health over time by taking regular assessments.
